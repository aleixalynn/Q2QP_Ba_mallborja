Initiatives

UNICEF works with governments to improve the quality of newborn, child, adolescent and maternal health care - especially at the community level. We do this through the development of cultural and age-appropriate health protocols and guidelines.


Involvements

UNICEF works in over 190 countries and territories to save children's lives, to defend their rights, and to help them fulfil their potential, from early childhood through adolescence.


Activities and Participation

  • Child Protection and Inclusion
  • Child Survival
  • Education
  • Social Policy
  • UNICEF in Emergencies
  • Gender
  • Innovation for Children
  • Research, evidence and analysis
